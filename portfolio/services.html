Micropigmentation Eyebrow Techniques

Ombre Powder Brows

Ombre powder brows are a permanent make up treatment that uses a hand piece device to insert tiny dots of pigment into the skin. It creates a more shaded effect. The result is a more defined, fuller brow, similar to the look you can achieve using cosmetic eyebrow powders or pencils.

Book Now

Nano Hair Strokes

Nano hair strokes is a technique that mimics the natural hair following the harmony of the brows. With a tiny needle the pigment is inserted in the second layer of the skin.

Book Now

Hybrid Brows

A Hybrid Brow, or fusion/combo brow is a combination of micropigmentation and microblading. Micropigmentation is a technique done with a digital machine which implants pigment a little deeper in the skin creating a very soft and owdery look after healing is complete.
